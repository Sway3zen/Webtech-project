work steps

1

Brief + contract

Send us your brief for your shoot so we can give you an individual offer with all the details. Once we find an agreement we draft a contract for your approval.

2

pre-production

We start to create an idea, write a script based on your preferences or making mood board. Find a cast, best locations, schedule the process and prepare for the shoot.

3

production

The process of shooting with our team, models and the client who can be also there for making some changes in a process if it’s needed. There are basically 2 locations for shooting: our studio or any other place which is outside.

4

post-production

Editing proceeds as follows: we make first variant for editing and send it to the client for checking and amends. The second variant we send for client’s final approval.
